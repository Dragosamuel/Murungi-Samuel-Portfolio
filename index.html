About Me

My Journey of Faith

My name is Murungi Samuel, born in Hoima City, Western Uganda, at Booma Referral Hospital. I grew up in the Western Uganda Field of the Seventh-day Adventist Church, where my spiritual journey began at the age of three.

I was nurtured in the Adventurer Club, later advanced to the Pathfinder Club, and learned the values of obedience, teamwork, and leadership â€” principles that have shaped my Christian walk. Our family later moved to Fort Portal City, where I continued to serve for nine years at the SDA Church Kagote.

When we returned to Hoima, where the Field Headquarters were established, I continued serving under the mentorship of my parents, Pr. Samuel Rwabuyaga and Mrs. Juliet Rwabuyaga, who guided me faithfully and shaped my ministry.

Later, I enrolled for the Master Guide program in the Western Uganda Field, graduating with a renewed commitment to youth ministry and mission work. I later joined the North Buganda Field, where I trained as a Senior Youth Leader at Bugema University, graduating into the Senior Youth Family.

Mission

To mentor, inspire, and equip young people to serve God faithfully, develop strong character, and become leaders of integrity and influence in the church and community.

Vision

To raise a generation of Adventist youth who are spiritually strong, socially responsible, and fully devoted to God's mission.

"Don't let anyone look down on you because you are young, but set an example for the believers in speech, in conduct, in love, in faith, and in purity."

My Ministry Journey

Over the years, God has opened doors for me to serve in different regions and fields across the Uganda Union and beyond.

Service Across Uganda

I have travelled to various districts, churches, youth camps, schools, and mission fields, sharing the gospel and mentoring young people.

Through these missions, I have been blessed to guide many Adventurers, Pathfinders, Ambassadors, Young Adults, and Master Guides in their spiritual growth and leadership development.

Certifications & Recognition

I have been awarded several leadership certifications from the Uganda Union Mission and additional certifications from the North American Division of the Seventh-day Adventist Church, strengthening my skills in youth leadership and Christian service.
